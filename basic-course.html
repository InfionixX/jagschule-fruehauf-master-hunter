Basic Hunter Certification

8-Week Comprehensive Introduction to Hunting

Our flagship course provides everything you need to begin your journey as an ethical, responsible hunter. Perfect for beginners with no prior experience.

Course Highlights

  • Firearms safety and handling
  • Wildlife identification and biology
  • Basic tracking and fieldcraft
  • Hunting laws and ethics
  • Game preparation and butchering

Course Details

Duration: 8 weeks (Mondays & Thursdays, 6-9pm)

Location: Jagdschule Frühauf Campus, Bavaria

Price: €1,200 (includes all materials and range fees)

Certification: European Hunter Certification upon completion

What You'll Learn

Week 1-2: Fundamentals

  • • Firearm safety rules and handling
  • • Introduction to different hunting weapons
  • • Marksmanship basics and range practice

Week 3-4: Wildlife Knowledge

  • • Game species identification
  • • Animal behavior and tracking
  • • Understanding habitats and ecosystems

Week 5-6: Field Skills

  • • Basic survival and navigation
  • • Setting up hunting blinds
  • • Field dressing and game preparation

Week 7-8: Practical Application

  • • Supervised hunting excursions
  • • Legal and ethical considerations
  • • Final assessment and certification
